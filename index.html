Stories School

Kazakh-Russian Specialized Lyceum School No. 54 named after Hero of the Soviet Union Major General Ivan Vasilievich Panfilov (School No. 54) is a lyceum school located in Alma-Ata (Kazakhstan).
In 1938, secondary school No. 54 was opened in the Leninsky district of Alma-Ata. In 1941, a military hospital was organized on the basis of the school, and on May 8, 1942, by decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Council of the Kazakh SSR, the educational institution was named after the Hero of the Soviet Union, Major General Ivan Vasilyevich Panfilov.
In 1995, the gymnasium became the winner of the competition within the framework of the program "Renewal of Humanitarian Education" conducted by the Soros Foundation - Kazakhstan. The gymnasium becomes the winner in the competition for the award of the Akim of the city.

In 2007, the gymnasium was the winner of the republican competition of scientific projects in chemistry on the topic "Polymeric forms of the antibiotic rifampicin based on alginate microparticles modified with chitosan." Finalist of the city competition "The Best Teacher of the Year 2007" (Zemtsova G. S.).
