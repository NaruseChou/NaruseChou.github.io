Kazakh-Russian Specialized Lyceum School No. 54 named after Hero of the Soviet Union Major General Ivan Vasilievich Panfilov (School No. 54) is a lyceum school located in Alma-Ata (Kazakhstan).
In 1938, secondary school No. 54 was opened in the Leninsky district of Alma-Ata. In 1941, a military hospital was organized on the basis of the school, and on May 8, 1942, by decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Council of the Kazakh SSR, the educational institution was named after the Hero of the Soviet Union, Major General Ivan Vasilyevich Panfilov.
In 1995, the gymnasium became the winner of the competition within the framework of the program "Renewal of Humanitarian Education" conducted by the Soros Foundation - Kazakhstan. The gymnasium becomes the winner in the competition for the award of the Akim of the city.

In 2007, the gymnasium was the winner of the republican competition of scientific projects in chemistry on the topic "Polymeric forms of the antibiotic rifampicin based on alginate microparticles modified with chitosan." Finalist of the city competition "The Best Teacher of the Year 2007" (Zemtsova G. S.).

New UNT 2022 format In 2021, 145 thousand graduates of Kazakhstan passed the unified national testing (UNT). According to the press service of the Ministry of Education and Science, according to the results of testing, more than 88 thousand applicants scored the threshold score, which is about 60% of the total number of participants. The threshold score was not passed by 57,187 test-takers, or 39.4% of their number. The maximum score (140) was scored by an applicant from Pavlodar. 1923 applicants scored over 120 points. 109,693 graduates (75.5% of applicants) were tested in the Kazakh language, 35,245 graduates (24.3%) - in Russian. The average UNT score was 69.08 points, which is five points higher than last year. Now school graduates and their parents are very worried about how the state exams (final certification of graduates) and UNT Unified National Testing) will be held in 2022. In this article, we want to give an explanation about the UNT 2022 format. The Unified National Examination UNT is a system for assessing knowledge in 5 subjects, the results of which are used as entrance exams to higher educational institutions of the Republic of Kazakhstan. The UNT is not mandatory for everyone. Graduates who do not plan to enter Kazakhstani universities, but choose a college, secondary specialized education, do not enter at all this year or enter universities in other countries, may not be tested. The Black Cloud, written in 1957 about the near future of 1964, now seems like true vintage fiction - with photographic plates, radio communications, television as an advanced mode of communication, but it is perhaps one of the most interesting and unorthodox books of the classical sai era. -Fai about the collision of humanity with a fundamentally different alien mind. The greatest pleasure, however, is provided by intellectual ping-pong between charming heroes who try to prevent an impending cosmic catastrophe solely with the help of science, while simultaneously overcoming the skepticism of politicians. It still seems to me that there is no better inspiration for choosing a scientific career.

CLIFFORD SAIMAK series of short stories "City" The "city" begins in the near future, and ends almost a thousand years later, when the last people entered the suspended animation stage, never to get out of it again, because they were tired of living. During this time, absolutely everything that is possible has time to happen. At the same time, the book surprisingly combines a humanistic message, the belief that people will eventually lose the battle with themselves, and humor. In one story, people fly off to explore Jupiter by transplanting their brains into bodies that mimic local animals. And they find out that their life is much better than human. In another, animals acquire the ability to speak and create their own society, in which a person enters on an equal footing with everyone else. It is also worth noting that all the stories tell about members of the same family, and the descendants of their dogs comment on the whole story (this is not a typo, really dogs, Canis lupus familliaris).
